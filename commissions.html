The Process

How It Works

Get in Touch

Send a message with your idea. Whether it's a photo of a loved one, a pet, or a concept you have in mind, every commission starts with a conversation.

Reference & Planning

We'll discuss the details: size, medium, composition, and timeline. I'll let you know what reference photos work best and walk you through the options.

Creation

I'll share progress photos along the way so you can see the piece develop. Each portrait takes between two and six weeks depending on complexity.

Delivery

Your finished artwork is carefully packaged and shipped to your door. Pieces are delivered unframed unless otherwise arranged.
