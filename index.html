The Artist

I’m Esme Lillico.

I work in charcoal and graphite because nothing else gives me the same range - from the deepest black to the softest whisper of grey, every tone between light and shadow is there to be found. That range lets me chase what I'm really after: the quiet, unguarded moments that reveal who someone is.

Every portrait I draw is a conversation between mark and meaning. I'm drawn to identity, intimacy, and the stories that sit just beneath the surface - a glance held a beat too long, the weight of a resting hand, the way light falls across a face when no one thinks they're being watched.
